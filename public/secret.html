Secret Formula Krabby Patty

Yields: 4 servings

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 10-12 minutes

Ingredients:

For the patties:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the burgers:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions:

Prepare the patties:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, onion, green bell pepper, egg, breadcrumbs, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix gently until just combined.

Shape the patties:

  1. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties about 1/2 inch thick.

Cook the patties:

  1. Heat a grill or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the patties for 5-7 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  3. If adding cheese, place a slice on top of each patty for the last minute of cooking.

Assemble the burgers:

  1. Toast the hamburger buns if desired.
  2. Place a cooked patty on the bottom bun, top with cheese (if using), lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
  3. Place the top bun on top.

Serve:

Enjoy your delicious homemade burgers!
